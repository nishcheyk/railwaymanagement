Railway Reservation System

The Railway Reservation System is designed to automate the online ticket purchasing process. It provides an easy-to-use platform to book tickets for various routes and destinations. With this system, you can manage reservations, client data, and passenger lists. You can also schedule routes, set seat availability, and let customers select their seats.


The system includes features like:

  • Easy & convenient booking
  • Manage reservations & seating effectively
  • Safe & secure payment gateway
  • Detail reports for managing trips
